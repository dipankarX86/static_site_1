Goods Elevators

Goods Elevator (also called a Freight Elevator or Cargo Lift) is an elevator specifically designed to carry heavy loads, materials, or goods rather than passengers. These are installed in buildings, warehouses, factories, or commercial spaces that is engineered with higher load capacity, durable construction, and larger cabin space to safely transport goods vertically between floors.

Gearless Machine for MRL Elevators

In Machine-Room-Less (MRL) elevators, there is no separate machine room above or below the shaft. Instead, the elevator’s hoisting machine is installed within the shaft itself, usually at the top or side. The gearless machine used here is a compact, high-efficiency traction motor (often Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor – PMSM) that directly drives the traction sheave without using a gearbox.

Hydraulic Elevator

Hydraulic Elevator is a type of lift that is powered by a piston moving inside a cylinder, which is driven by hydraulic fluid (oil). Instead of using ropes and counterweights like traction elevators, it uses fluid pressure to raise and lower the elevator car. These have High load capacity (great for goods and hospitals), Lower initial cost than traction elevators & No need for overhead structural space

Capsule Elevators

Capsule Elevator (also called a Panoramic Elevator) is a passenger lift with a glass cabin designed to give an aesthetic appeal and allow riders to view the surroundings while traveling between floors. They Enhances aesthetics and luxury value of the building, Provides panoramic view during travel, Comfortable, smooth, and quiet operation.
